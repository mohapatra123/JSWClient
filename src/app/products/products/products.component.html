DSW Products

Chatbot

A friendly chatbot backed by state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing that learns from the simple FAQs which also supports casual conversations and abusive language detection. Also provides major features like question suggestion when user’s question is not understood and log storage of individual user.

Face Recognition

A face recognition system developed using state-of-the-art deep learning techniques which supports a self-learning methodology that helps to improve performance. Recognizes face in microseconds even when scaled to thousands of users. Pretty much robust and requires minimal user data for effective recognition.

OCRWiz

An OCR utility backed by Computer Vision, NLP and Deep Learning. It’s an end-to-end pipeline from the recognition of the KYC documents to extracting unstructured text information to converting it into meaningful structured information. It handles rotated, skewed and average quality images without degrading the performance.

Smart Form

A smart form enabled with speech to text and text to speech utilities that enhance form filling journey by making it conversational. It will ease the process for the users by replacing the tedious manual form filling with interactive and conversational one thereby saving time and increasing the chances of conversion.

Call Center Analytics

Analytics to gain insights in an automated way using real- time Customer conversations to enhance business revenue by cross-selling & up-selling, prevent customer churn, improve customer satisfaction, assess the competency of the Customer-Representative call to measure agent performance, sentiment of words & analyse voice tone.

Video Consent

According to RBI’s amendment to master direction on KYC, RBI has allowed to get on-board remote customers via Video Based Customer Identification Process (V-CIP). Video Consent KYC enables banks & lending companies to perform customer on-boarding by taking the customer’s photo & identity proof over mobile video session with Geo-location.
